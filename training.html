Training Programs

Pilates Recover

Feel Great Again!

Reclaim your body after a traumatic injury with this program.


Clinical (or Rehabilitation) Pilates has been specifically developed for use by clinically trained professionals, so physiotherapists or Sports Therapists.

The exercises work to target the deep postural muscles of the tummy and spine. They also help improve spinal mobility, increase flexibility of the key trunk and leg muscle groups and improve body and postural awareness.

It helps to increase awareness of your body and improve your movement quality and control. It is particularly suitable for you if you suffer with back or neck pain or have suffered a specific injury or have a long standing condition such as arthritis and also a great way of preventing injuries or preparing for sport.

Strong Core!

You’ll get stronger, more sculpted muscles and gain flexibility. You will definitely have better posture and a better sense of well-being.


Pilates is great for strengthening and toning with a focus your core and for increasing your flexibility. However, you can expect to see strength gains in your arms and legs. Positions and movements used to activate core rely on extremities to control &/or apply loads to the core and likewise will benefit from Pilates.

In addition to working on bodily aspects, such as muscle toning, posture and physical conditioning, he also deals with the mental part of his practitioners, with breathing exercises, stretching and relaxation, which relieve anxiety and stress.
